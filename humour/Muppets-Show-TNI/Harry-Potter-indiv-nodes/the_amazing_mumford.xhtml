The Amazing Mumford

Node Link

[ Harry and Hermione go to see the Amazing Mumford, and notice his stage magic is indeed awful. After the first performance, they talk to him behind the stage, and understand that he is afraid to fail. So they tell him that it is OK if he fails, if people don’t like him too much, that even very experienced people fail some times, and that he should just be himself. After that his stage magic becomes much better. ]

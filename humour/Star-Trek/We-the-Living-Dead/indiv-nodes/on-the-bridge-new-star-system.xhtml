New Star System

Node Link

[ On the DS9 Bridge. Q, Sisko and Dax enter. Katie, Jake and Amanda are already there. ]

Officer: Captain, there’s a new star system a few thousands of lightyears away, and its sun has an irregular shape.

Sisko: What? Show it on the screen.

[ The Screen shows a yellow star shaped like the capital letter “Q” (in Sans-Serif) and some planets. ]

Sisko: I don’t believe it.

Q: Captain - that is the Q star system.

Sisko: I don’t suppose I should travel there on the Defiant.

Q: Not now. Allow me to teleport us.

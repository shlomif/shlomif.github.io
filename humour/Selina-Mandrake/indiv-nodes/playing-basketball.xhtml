Playing Basketball

Node Link

[ Selina, Matthew, Jessica and Jonathan are playing Basketball over one basket on a court. Selina is breathless. ]

Selina: Time out, time out [does a T with her hands.]

[ She breathes heavily. ]

Selina: Wow, I am getting killed here.

Jonathan: Crushed here!

Selina: Yeah, completely squashed here.

Matthew: Slain here.

[ Cut to Selina’s face. She seems startled. ]

Selina: Slain?

Matthew: Heh, yes, like a vampire is getting slain.

Selina: Tell you what? I’m not feeling too well. You can continue to play without me. Bye, I’m leaving. See you. Love you.

[ She walks away looking worried. ]

Selina’s internal dialogue: Wow, why me? Why me? This is maddening.

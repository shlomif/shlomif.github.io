Showing the rented apartment to a hot girl

When I was studying in the Technion, I corented a nearby apartment along with two other roommates. They decided to move after the first year and so we were looking for new housemates.

One girl who came to see the apartment:

  1. Was thin, but not underweight.

  2. Had a beautiful face reminiscent of Gabrielle Anwar.

  3. Wore a black sports bra as a top.

  4. Had blonde hair.

  5. Said she was going to study Electrical Engineering.

So she looked incredibly hot. I showed her the apartment, and answered her questions while being infatuated with her. I wasn't at my best element.

Later on, after I described her to one of the departing housemates, who was supposed to meet her next, he said "I'm going to await her…".

  • Eric Steven Raymond (also known as ESR) is a software engineer, consultant and writer.
  • He became famous after writing the essay "The Cathedral and the Bazaar" which was since then updated and followed up by other essays.
  • He is considered one of the most prominent leaders of the Open-Source/Free Software movement.
  • Some of the other interesting things by him are: the How to Become a Hacker FAQ, How to Ask Smart Questions, Software Release Practice HOWTO (how to launch a successful open-source project), and the HTML Hell Page.

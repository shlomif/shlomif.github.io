  • There are two types of frameworks called Content-Management Systems (or CMSes for short):
    1. Such that are installed on the server (as a dynamic extension), are configured somehow and are used to manage and customize the site.
    2. Such that are operated on the developer's local machine, generate a final static or partially dynamic site which is then uploaded as is to the server.

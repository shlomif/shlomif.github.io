1.2. Future Directions

ContentsUpPrevNext
Do it with the GIMP → Introduction → Future Directions
  • The development of GIMP 2.4 as part of the 2.3.x series has already began. It will be mostly backwards compatible to GIMP 2.2, and will feature some improvements. It is scheduled to a few months in the future.
  • For the more distant future, the GIMP developers are planning on working on the GEGL library, and making the GIMP use it as its core. This will allow support for advanced features such as CMYK support, 16-bits per color value, and color-spaces.

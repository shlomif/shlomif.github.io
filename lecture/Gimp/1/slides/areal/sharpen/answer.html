4.2.1. Answer to how one can sharpen

ContentsUpPrevNext
Do it with the GIMP → Basic Areal Effects → Sharpening → Answer to how one can sharpen
  • Sharpen is in effect the reverse of blurring.
  • We sharpen by negating some of the energy of the surrounding pixels from the pixel at the processed position.

Demo

  1. Open lena.png
  2. Invoke the "Convolution Matrix" dialog.
  3. Type 12 in the middle square and -1 in the 8 surrounding squares.
  4. Type 4 as a divisor (12 + 8 * (-1) == 4).
  5. Apply.

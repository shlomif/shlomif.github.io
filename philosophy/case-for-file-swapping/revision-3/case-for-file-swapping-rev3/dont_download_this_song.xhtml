Weird Al Yankovic’s “Don’t Download This Song”

Weird Al Yankovic, the famous musical comedian, has released the track “Don’t Download this Song” out of his album Straight outta Lynwood. Despite its ironic title, it is available for free download.

The Lyrics for the song are illuminating the absurdity of the big copyright owners’ fight against file-swapping in a funny way.

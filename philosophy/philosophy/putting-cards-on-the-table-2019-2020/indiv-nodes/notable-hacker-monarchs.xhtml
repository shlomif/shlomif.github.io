Some notable hacker monarchs

Node Link

Some examples for notable Hacker Monarchs, from my Jewish/Secular Israeli and Western (Near East/Europe/North Americas) bias, are:

  1. Moses

  2. Cyrus the Great

  3. Socrates

  4. Plato

  5. Aristotle (believed to have been a band of philosophers with a central poster boy figure)

  6. Qoheleth / Ecclesiastes

  7. ( Jesus may have been a hacker monarch, but I have not studied his teachings closely, and have some natural aversion towards Christianity as an Israeli Jew. )

  8. Muhammad

  9. Saladin

  10. Leonardo da Vinci

  11. Galileo Galilei

  12. Isaac Newton

  13. Cardinal Richelieu

  14. The American Revolutionaries

  15. Alexandre Dumas (pere)

  16. Jules Verne

  17. Walt Disney

  18. Ayn Rand

  19. The Beatles

  20. Sesame Street / Jim Henson / The Muppets

  21. Samantha Smith

  22. Richard Stallman / Larry Wall / Linus Torvalds and other Usenet / FOSS hackers with Eric S. Raymond writing the Magnus Opus CatB.

  23. The fictional Jean-Luc Picard

  24. The fictional Buffy Summers being the poster girl of "Web 1.0".

  25. Joel Spolsky / Eric S. Raymond / Paul Graham (programmer) / Lawrence Lessig / Shlomi Fish and other software developers and bloggers / web writers of the ~2000-2014 web 2.0/"open content" revolution.

  26. In Summerschool at the NSA, I believed Summer Glau and Xkcd will succeed me. While other "messiahs" / hacker-monarchs in the past tried to convince everyone else to consider themselves as such, it was only possible after the web became prevalent.

Following the Summer Glau "termination", it was believed that there now can be many simultaneous hacker monarchs, and that people can try to become ones while not dethroning (or sic transit gloria mundi) the previous hacker monarchs. “We don’t die. We multiply.”

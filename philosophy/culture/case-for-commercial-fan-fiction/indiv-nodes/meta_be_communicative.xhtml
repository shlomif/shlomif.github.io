Be communicative about changing your policy

Node Link

The MPAA should publicly announce their plans to also accept screenplays in new electronic formats with accessible linters, and to instruct their screenreaders to try be more communicative. Per Saladin's Ethics, I do not propose laying off the existing screenreaders.

I feel this is the current bottleneck, but the film studios should also approve of writing commercial crossovers, parodies and RPFs.

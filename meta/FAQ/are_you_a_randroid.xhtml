Does the fact that you were influenced by Objectivism (= Ayn Rand’s philosophy) make you a “Randroid”?

Node Link

The way I see it, a "Randroid" is an overzealous believer in Randianism, who mindlessly parrots Rand's works. On the other hand, my philosophy, "Rindolfism" is a crossover one with many influences, and dynamic. Moreover, I don't take Randianism as gospel, often deviate from it, and am critical of it. For example, I wrote a parody and modernisation of The Fountainhead, and also wrote Summerschool at the NSA, which I feel is a modernisation of Atlas Shrugged.

If you still want to label me as a "Randroid", go ahead - I won't care that much. Just note that it is you who are being stupid, prejudiced and labelling.

May I prepare a derivative of a screenplay, bit, aphorism, essay, captioned image, or other work (or works) of yours? (E.g: a computer game, a tabletop game, a parody, a video, an animation, an audio file, a picture, a version with some amendments or corrections, a translation to a different language?)

Node Link

Sure! Go ahead as I believe most of my works are under licences that allow derivatives given respecting certain conditions. Even if you violate the conditions, I may likely lack the time and energy to start a law suit and instead just gently "shame" you on Internet social media or other sites.

Also see some relicensing options.

Do note that I try to provide sources for most of my works - usually on my GitHub account or other code sharing sites (where "code" can be markup or a different file format). As a result, I recommend you peruse them if you wish to keep the best quality.
